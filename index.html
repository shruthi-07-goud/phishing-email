🛡️ Phishing Awareness Tips

📧 Check Sender

Always verify the sender's email address before trusting any email.

🔗 Avoid Unknown Links

Hover over links before clicking them.

🔑 Never Share Passwords

Legitimate companies never ask for passwords through email.

⚠️ Beware of Urgency

Scammers often create panic to trick users.

💳 Protect Banking Details

Never enter banking information from email links.

📎 Check Attachments

Avoid opening unexpected ZIP or EXE files.

About This Project

The Phishing Email Detection & Awareness System is a simple cybersecurity project developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It analyzes email content by checking for phishing indicators such as urgent language, suspicious links, password requests, banking keywords, fake domains, and malicious attachments. Based on the analysis, the email is classified as Safe, Suspicious, or Phishing, along with a risk score and security recommendations.
